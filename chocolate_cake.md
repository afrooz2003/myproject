
# Chocolate Cake

## Ingredients
- 1 cup flour  
- 1 cup sugar  
- ½ cup cocoa powder  
- 1 tsp baking soda  
- ½ tsp salt  
- 1 cup water  
- ⅓ cup oil  
- 1 tsp vanilla  
- 1 tbsp vinegar

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).  
2. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.  
3. Add wet ingredients and stir until smooth.  
4. Pour into a greased 8-inch pan.  
5. Bake for 30–35 minutes. Let cool.
